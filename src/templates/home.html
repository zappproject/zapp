Send Bitcoin with only a Username.

Mass adoption depends on ease of use. The Zapp platform addresses this concern by allowing for instant transactions using human usernames, making sending bitcoin easier than an username. Your desktop or hardware wallet serves as your personal "Bank", whilst everyday transactions are made on the Zapp platform for convenience. The easiest way to harness the power of Bitcoin.

Easy to use

Bitcoin without the jargon.

Send bitcoin like you would an username. No complicated numbers instead utilize simple understandable usernames like "mywallet".

Zapp is free to use, and will always be a platform for instantaeneous, secure transactions and withdrawals.

Micro-Payments

Micropayment Capabilities

Zapp's no credit card, no fee transactions makes it ideal for instant worldwide micropayments to anyone you want.

Using Zapp

Easiest way to transfer money online using Bitcoin in just 4 steps.

Step 1: Make an Account. No username required, Just a creative, available username.

Step 2: Deposit BTC from your current wallet into the Zapp Account. Current deposits for the beta are topped at 10usd for security purposes.

Step 3: Send BTC to whoever you want for free using just their username. Attach a message to each transaction too.

Step 4: Whenever you please, btc is automatically withdrawn to your wallet instantly, for no fee's.
